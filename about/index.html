About Crown Plumbing Inc

Crown Plumbing Inc was established in 2005 and serves residential and commercial plumbing needs for all New York City boroughs including Manhattan, Brooklyn, Bronx, Queens, and Staten Island. We also service select areas of New Jersey.

Our boutique plumbing company provides a personal level service unrivaled by others. Our expert plumbers can help you fix anything from leaky pipes to complete remodeling jobs.

Customer satisfaction is always guaranteed, and we work hard to provide the best service possible. We have consulted with professional contractors, engineers, and registered architects to ensure that jobs are done to the highest specification possible.


About Our Founder John Mirzo

John is a New York City Licensed Master Plumber (License #2253). He has worked as a plumber for over fifteen years and truly cares about providing the best service to customers. He founded Crown Plumbing Inc to be able to provide a more personalized level of service.
